Designing for educators brings with it it's own set of unique challenges. All educators share in common that they need to be able to communicate with their students and facilitate learning. That aside, educators are an incredibly diverse cast of individuals. An issue with many ed-tech companies is that their educator personas are far too monolithic. Educators are as diverse and unique as students are.
